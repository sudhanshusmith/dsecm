Objective of the DST CoE

Objective of the DST CoE

Our team is carrying out required modifications to an existing global climate model to develop a better model for the Indian region through improved physical and computational implementations. Specifically, we are developing the ICCM through process improvements and region-specific customization for more accurate simulations of the regional climate over the Indian region. Our model will be used for future climate projections at district level to assess sectoral impacts of climate change on agriculture, health, water resources, and energy. Furthermore, we will downscale the ICCM outputs at ultra-high resolutions for various applications. Additional climate projections for future scenarios will be made by combining the ICCM projections with soft computing, machine learning, and mathematical optimization techniques. In addition to the research objectives, our CoE has been playing a leading role in training and nurturing future climate modelers of India.
